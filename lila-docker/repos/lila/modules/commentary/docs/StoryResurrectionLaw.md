# Story Resurrection Law

Prepared on 2026-05-05 for every BoardMood slot classified as `버림`.

## Law

BoardMood does not create, score, revive, or hint at the meanings below. Each
listed slot remains `0`/silent in BoardMood. A public chess idea may be spoken
only by a Story that binds side, target, anchor, route, rival, required legal
line, and same-root proof sidecar as required by the row. If the row requires
an engine line or a move sequence, the Story must carry same-root evidence
before wording is allowed. A forgeable numeric `Proof` score is not enough.

Hash rows never become public chess ideas. They may identify a position for
internal equality checks, but they do not support a chess sentence.

Every row inherits mandatory Story binding: side, target, anchor, route, rival,
required legal line, and same-root proof sidecar. The table's required proof
identity column adds row-specific chess identity; it never replaces the
mandatory binding tuple. If a row cell names only the chess identities that are
special to that row, the omitted binding fields are still required and must be
concrete before public wording is allowed.

## Fail-Closed Rules

- Missing side, target, anchor, route, rival, required legal line, or same-root proof sidecar means no public sentence.
- A forgeable numeric `Proof` score is not enough.
- Engine context does not speak by itself; it must be attached to the Story row
  identity above.
- Legal lines must use legal moves from the current position and must name the
  move path they support.
- Public wording must stay inside the row limit even when several proofs are
  present.
